# HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

A comprehensive HR Analytics Dashboard designed to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, compensation trends, and job role performance using Power BI.It provides a comprehensive view of employee turnover, performance, and satisfaction metrics across different departments.

## Overview

This dashboard provides a structured and interactive analysis of employee attrition across departments, job roles, salary slabs, age groups, and tenure.

## Objectives
The objective is to help HR teams identify high-risk attrition segments and support data-driven workforce planning decisions.
- Measure overall attrition rate and workforce stability
- Identify departments and job roles with highest turnover
- Analyze the impact of age and tenure on attrition
- Understand salary slab distribution among exiting employees
- Examine gender-based attrition trends
